My Music Journey

  • Discovering Music

    My journey in music began in elementary school with learning the ukulele. I played with a sense of curiosity and found myself drawn to the freedom of musical expression. The ukulele introduced me to the pure beauty of sound, opening a door into the world of music and sparking my enduring passion.

  • Joining the Recorder Team

    As I grew older, I started learning the recorder and joined the school team. This experience taught me the importance of teamwork and precision in rhythm, allowing me to appreciate the harmony of playing together. It not only improved my skills but also helped me understand music as a form of connection.

  • Junior High Music Class and Starting Composition

    In junior high, I entered the music class and began studying piano and clarinet. In my second year, my teacher discovered my potential in composition and suggested I change my focus. This opened a new path for me, allowing me to express my thoughts and emotions through music, which has had a profound impact on me.

  • High School Music Program and Studying Abroad

    After graduating from junior high, I joined the music program at Zhongli High School. Now a sophomore, I aim to study abroad to deepen my musical journey. This path holds great meaning for me
